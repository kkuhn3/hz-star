Highlander Zero Star Chips
Custom format designed for Mega Man Battle Network 1 through 6. Build a folder of 30 unique chips, without having access to star chips. Express your own style to delete those you face.
High level rules:
Singleton format, meaning only one of each chip by name allowed in ones folder.
Star Chips, seen as: in the custom screen, are not allowed.
